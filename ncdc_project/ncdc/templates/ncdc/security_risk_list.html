Security Risk Assessment and Management Framework

As part of requirements for project effectiveness, the Nigeria CoPREP was required to develop and disclose a security risk assessment and management framework to guide the deployment of security operatives (Nigeria Police Force and Nigeria Civil Defence Corps) as part of the vaccination team during vaccine deployment across the 36 states and FCT. The safeguard team worked with Spearfish Security West Africa Limited to conduct the assessment and develop the framework which is available in the link below. The documents are to be disclosed to guide participating states to develop their site-specific security management plans and to sensitize other stakeholders.
